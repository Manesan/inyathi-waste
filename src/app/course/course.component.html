Why Recycle?

Through recycling we can create an environment that is eco-friendly and that is filled with opportunities for all.

The advantages of recycling are infinite; and here are few:

↻  Reduce waste

↻ Reduce water pollution

↻ Save energy

↻ Protect wildlife

↻ Creation of jobs and opportunities

↻ To reuse and reduce the depletion of natural resources

Inyathi waste is dedicated to becoming a fundamental driver towards sustainable development and in doing so, we create a greener future for all.
