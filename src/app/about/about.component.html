Why Inyathi Waste?

keyboard_arrow_right Recycle today for a better tomorrow

At Inyathi Waste, we want to revolutionise recycling by introducing innovative waste management solutions, to ensure that all recyclable waste is extracted. Our main goal is to rejuvenate the environment allowing us to live in a greener future for all. We strive to maintain and promote growth and sustainable solutions through our effective waste management techniques.

keyboard_arrow_rightAdaptable Solutions

Our intuitive waste management solutions are tailor made for each company no matter the size or sector. We ensure that waste is separated source, allowing us to guarantee all recyclable waste is recycled. Through our innovative solutions we ensure that less waste makes its way to landfills.

keyboard_arrow_rightHazardous and non-hazardous waste

Waste comes in different shapes and sizes, not all waste is equal. All waste is sorted out into categories which allow for ethical and responsible disposal. Sustainable solutions are developed where possible.
